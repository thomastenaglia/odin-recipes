Lasagna

Description

This traditional Lasagna Recipe, is a simple lasagna from the Region of Lazio in Italy. A delicious Homemade sauce makes this one of the best! The perfect family or company coming dinner idea. Comfort food never tasted so good.

Lasagna, comes in so many different types and they’re all so good! There are many ways of making it. I have to admit I have eaten quite a few types from, Zucchini, White Sauce, a Stovetop Lasaga and even an Asparagus Lasagna and of course this Traditional Lasagna. If you want to make it even richer make it with this Bolognese Sauce.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Pound of Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Celery Stalk
  • Garlic
  • peppers
  • Lasagna Strips
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot add the olive oil, ground beef, chopped carrots, celery, onion, garlic and salt, sauté for approximately 10 minutes or until meat has started to brown, then add the tomato pure, water and spices, cook on medium heat until thickened (quite thick), approximately 30-60 minutes.
  2. In a baking dish measuring 10x10x2 (25x25x5 cm) spread 2-3 spoonfuls of meat sauce on the bottom of the pan, add a layer of lasagna noodles, then continue to layer in the following order, the meat sauce, white sauce, chopped mozzarella and parmesan cheese, continue making 3-4 layers, the last layer should be without the white sauce, so remember to divide the ingredients by 3 or 4, depending on how many layers you wish to make.
  3. Bake for approximate 20-30 minutes, or until cooked through. Let sit 5-10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
